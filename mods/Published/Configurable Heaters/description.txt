[h1]Configurable Heaters[/h1]
This mod adds configuration options for Space Heater and Liquid Tepedizer

[img]https://i.imgur.com/xC5HrQ0.png[/img]

[b]Setting Heating to negative number will cause them to cool gases/liquids untli set temperature is reached[/b]
